Experience by Expertise

My work focuses on using advanced research methods to prove the value and effectiveness of healthcare services through financial and clinical outcomes.

  • I have leveraged causal inference to quantify cost savings by analyzing medical claims data from two distinct populations: members who used our product and a comparable cohort who did not.
  • My research demonstrated that engaged members achieved significant savings in community healthcare utilization, including reductions in urgent care visits, hospitalizations, and specialty visits.
  • My analysis and key insights were presented to senior leadership and directly influenced major strategic decisions
  • By proving the value of our product, I have helped defend/renew multi-million dollar contracts with my analytical findings.
  • I have also measured the impact of treatments on clinical outcomes for chronic condition populations, such as diabetics (lowering A1c), obesity (reducing BMI), hypertension (lowering blood pressure), and mental health conditions (improving PHQ-9 and GAD-7 scores).
  • With extensive daily experience in medical data, I utilize a comprehensive knowledge of medical claims, diagnosis codes, CPT codes, HEDIS, and EMRs to inform product and experimental design.

In my role, the patient's care journey the product. Many of my daily responsibilities within the Product team focus on improving our delivery of healthcare, whether it be through efficiencies at inidividual clinics, new features on our website/app, or better healthcare outcomes. At the core, we use data to power our understanding of the patient exprience.

  • I have led comprehensive funnel and cohort analyses to inform business strategy by mapping the member journey from acquisition through their entire course of care to identify drop-off points.

  • I process and analyze patient satisfaction survey data, in order to understand the overall sentiment towards our individual clinics and providers, as well as our services as a whole.
