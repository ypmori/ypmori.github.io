Professional Experience

Healthcare & Causal Inference

Specializing in proving the financial and clinical value of healthcare interventions. I design and execute advanced causal inference studies (e.g., Difference-in-Differences, Propensity Score Matching) to quantify multi-million dollar ROI for major enterprise clients like Amazon and Meta. My analyses demonstrate significant cost savings and improved clinical outcomes by comparing engaged member populations against matched control groups using extensive medical claims data.

  • Quantified cost savings by analyzing reductions in urgent care visits, hospitalizations, and specialty care utilization.
  • Measured clinical outcome improvements for chronic condition populations (Diabetes, Hypertension, Obesity, Mental Health) by tracking key biomarkers like A1c, BMI, and PHQ-9 scores.
  • Leveraged deep knowledge of medical data, including diagnosis codes (ICD-10), CPT codes, HEDIS measures, and EMR data to build robust patient cohorts for analysis.
